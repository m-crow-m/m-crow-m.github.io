JAEDEN

My work is an attempt to organize the thoughts, ideas, themes, messages, and chaos that exists in all of our projects. I seek to guide the viewer through the experience of viewing a website, a poster, or any graphic medium. I treat projects as ongoing, there will never be an end to the coming and passing of trends, there will never be an end to the advancement of the software we use and the invention of new ones; but with some effort, we can produce something that is beautiful and that may, perhaps, transcend the time.
